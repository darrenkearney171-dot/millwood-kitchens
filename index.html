📐 How to sketch your kitchen measurements

You don't need to be an architect — a clear hand-drawn sketch is all we need. Follow the steps on the right.

Example sketch — draw yours on plain paper and photograph it

Follow these steps when drawing your sketch:

  1. 1
    Draw the floor plan outline — a simple rectangle or L-shape viewed from above.
  2. 2
    Label walls A, B, C, D going clockwise from the main kitchen wall.
  3. 3
    Measure each wall in millimetres at worktop height (~900mm from floor).
  4. 4
    Mark every window with its width and sill height from the floor.
  5. 5
    Mark every door — which wall, hinge side, and swing direction.
  6. 6
    Note radiators, boiler, soil pipes, sockets and switches — anything fixed that can't be moved.
  7. 7
    Measure ceiling height and write it clearly on the sketch.
  8. 8
    Photograph the sketch in good light and upload it below with your room photos.
