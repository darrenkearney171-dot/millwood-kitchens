Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering your Millwood kitchen.

Is the estimate really free?+
Yes — 100% free with no obligation. Whether you use our online form, upload your plans, or speak to us by phone, your itemised kitchen estimate costs nothing. We believe in transparency: you see every unit, door, hinge, and handle individually priced before you commit to anything.
What’s the difference between the free estimate and the paid design service?+
The free estimate gives you an itemised price list based on the kitchen size, style, and options you choose. Our optional Home Survey (£200) includes a designer visiting your home to take precise measurements, while the Full 3D Design Service (£250) adds photorealistic renders so you can see exactly how your kitchen will look. Both paid services are redeemable against your order.
How long does delivery take?+
Standard delivery is 3–4 weeks from order confirmation. We manufacture everything at our Northern Ireland factory, so there are no long import waits. Delivery is available UK-wide, and we’ll coordinate a convenient date with you.
Do you supply worktops and appliances?+
We supply worktops in laminate, solid timber, and stone-effect finishes — all included in our estimates. For appliances, we partner with leading brands and can quote these separately, or you’re welcome to source your own.
Do you offer installation?+
We focus on manufacturing and supply. However, we can recommend trusted installers in your area who are experienced with our kitchens. Many customers also use their own builder or kitchen fitter.
What guarantee do I get?+
Every Millwood kitchen comes with our 10-year guarantee covering manufacturing defects on all cabinets, doors, and hardware. We stand behind the quality of everything we make.

📐 How to sketch your kitchen measurements

You don't need to be an architect — a clear hand-drawn sketch is all we need. Follow the steps on the right.

Example sketch — draw yours on plain paper and photograph it

Follow these steps when drawing your sketch:

  1. 1
    Draw the floor plan outline — a simple rectangle or L-shape viewed from above.
  2. 2
    Label walls A, B, C, D going clockwise from the main kitchen wall.
  3. 3
    Measure each wall in millimetres at worktop height (~900mm from floor).
  4. 4
    Mark every window with its width and sill height from the floor.
  5. 5
    Mark every door — which wall, hinge side, and swing direction.
  6. 6
    Note radiators, boiler, soil pipes, sockets and switches — anything fixed that can't be moved.
  7. 7
    Measure ceiling height and write it clearly on the sketch.
  8. 8
    Photograph the sketch in good light and upload it below with your room photos.

          What data we collect

          When you submit an estimate request, we collect: your name, email address, phone number, postcode, and details about your kitchen requirements including room measurements and photos you choose to upload. We use this information solely to prepare and deliver your kitchen estimate.

          How we use your data

          We use your personal data to: prepare your kitchen estimate, contact you about your enquiry, and improve our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

          Data storage

          Your data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above. Form submissions are processed through Netlify’s secure form handling service.

          Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@millwoodkitchens.com.

          This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We use only essential cookies required for the website to function.
