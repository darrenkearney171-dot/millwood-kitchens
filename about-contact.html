Kitchens designed around you

I've been working in kitchens and joinery for over 10 years in Northern Ireland.

I started Millwood Kitchens because I saw the same thing happen time and again — homeowners sitting in showrooms under pressure, buying kitchens that looked great in the display but never quite worked in their actual space.

The Millwood model is different. I come to your home with professional design software, take your exact measurements, and design your kitchen in front of you — in your actual room, with your actual walls, your actual windows and your actual lifestyle in mind.

You walk away with a complete kitchen design and a full itemised quote before I leave. No pressure. No chasing. No surprises.

Every kitchen I design is supplied and installed by experienced tradespeople I trust. Our units and doors come from P&A Contracts — an established Northern Ireland manufacturer — so you're getting locally made, quality product at a genuinely fair price.

If you're planning a new kitchen and want to know what's possible at a realistic price, I'd love to hear from you.

— Millwood Kitchens
