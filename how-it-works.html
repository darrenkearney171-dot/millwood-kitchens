How It Works

From your first enquiry to your finished kitchen — here's exactly what to expect.

1

Step 1 — Get a free estimate

Start by submitting your kitchen plans, measurements or a rough hand-drawn sketch using our free estimate form. Tell us the size of your kitchen, your rough budget and when you're hoping to have it done.

Darren will review your submission and come back to you within 48 hours with a realistic ballpark cost. This is completely free and there's no obligation to proceed.

If the estimate works for you, the next step is booking your in-home design visit.

Get a Free Estimate →
2

Step 2 — Book your in-home design visit — £150

The design visit is £150, paid securely online when you book — this is what holds your slot in the diary.

Darren arrives at your home at the agreed time with a laptop running ArtiCAD — the same professional kitchen design software used by kitchen studios. He takes precise measurements of your kitchen space and talks through everything: the layout you want, door style, colours, handles, worktop, appliances.

Then your kitchen is designed live, in front of you. You can see it build on screen in real time. Don't like something? It changes on the spot. The visit typically takes 2–3 hours.

At the end of the visit you receive a full itemised quote and your kitchen plans — printed and emailed. No obligation to proceed. No chasing. No waiting.

The £150 visit fee is fully deducted from your deposit if you decide to go ahead.

Book Your Design Visit →
3

Step 3 — Confirm your order

Happy with the design and quote? We confirm your order with a 50% deposit — your £150 visit fee is already deducted from this.

We order your kitchen through our manufacturing partner P&A Contracts, an established Northern Ireland manufacturer. EGGER carcasses, Blum soft-close hardware, and doors manufactured to your exact specification.

Lead time is typically 4–6 weeks from order confirmation to installation, depending on your specification.

4

Step 4 — Supply and professional installation

Our installation team arrives on the agreed date and fits your kitchen to the highest standard.

We handle everything — unit assembly, door hanging, worktop templating and fitting, sink connection and appliance integration.

On completion, you pay the remaining balance and enjoy your new kitchen. That's it.

Common questions

What areas do you cover?

We cover all of Northern Ireland. If you're just outside our usual area, get in touch and we'll let you know if we can help.

What makes Millwood different from a kitchen showroom?

We come to you — no showroom visit needed. We keep our overheads low so our prices are genuinely competitive. And because we design in your actual kitchen space, the design always fits your room perfectly. We also handle supply and installation — one team, one point of contact throughout.

Who manufactures your kitchens?

Our kitchens are manufactured in partnership with P&A Contracts, an established Northern Ireland manufacturer. We supply EGGER carcasses with Blum soft-close drawer and hinge systems, with doors manufactured to your exact specification. Worktops from leading UK suppliers.

Is the £150 design visit fee really refundable?

Yes — fully. If you go ahead with your kitchen order, the £150 is deducted from your deposit. It only costs you anything if you decide not to proceed.

How long does a design visit take?

Typically 2–3 hours depending on the size and complexity of your kitchen.

Do you supply and install, or just supply?

Both. We handle the full process — design, supply and professional installation. You don't need to find or manage a separate fitter.

Can I change the design after the visit?

Yes. Minor changes are free. If the design changes significantly, a second visit may be required.

Ready to get started?

Not sure on budget yet? Get a free estimate first — no obligation. Ready to go? Book your design visit and we'll be with you within the week.
